## Maker's First Comment (draft)

Hi Product Hunt! I'm Aaron, and I built First Light because I was tired of opening my task manager and feeling *more* overwhelmed than before I looked.

The idea started simply: what if my app wrote me a morning briefing instead of showing me a list? Not bullet points — an actual narrative about my day. Something that reads like a newspaper column.

"An open day asks its own kind of question. Nothing is scheduled. Nothing is due. The morning is yours to shape."

That's a real Daily Edition from First Light. It's written by AI, but it reads like a human editor who knows your schedule.

**What it does:**
- Tasks, calendar sync, habits, Pomodoro — the essentials
- **Daily Edition** — an AI morning briefing that edits your day into a readable narrative
- **Weekly Retro** — an end-of-week reflection written in your voice
- **5 languages** with real typographic care: EN, 繁體中文, 简体中文, 日本語, 한국어

**What it doesn't do:**
- Team features (it's personal, by design)
- Gamification (no streaks, no points, no guilt)
- Noise (no red badges, no push notifications about drinking water)

I built this as a solo maker alongside my full-time job in Taipei. It's free to start, with Plus ($5/mo) and Pro ($9/mo) for AI features.

Would love your feedback — especially on the Daily Edition. That's the part I'm most proud of.

→ [firstlight.to](https://firstlight.to)
