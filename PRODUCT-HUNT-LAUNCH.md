## Description (260 chars)
First Light is a calm daily planner that writes you a personal morning briefing. It looks at your tasks, calendar, and patterns, then writes an editorial about your day — what matters, what can wait, what you're forgetting. Free to start. EN/中文/日本語/한국어.

## Maker's First Comment (final)

Hi Product Hunt! I'm Aaron, and I built First Light because I was tired of opening my task manager and feeling *more* overwhelmed than before I looked.

The idea started simply: what if my app wrote me a morning briefing instead of showing me a list? Not bullet points — an actual narrative about my day. Something that reads like a newspaper column written by someone who knows me.

**What it does:**
- **Daily Edition** — an AI morning briefing that edits your day into a readable narrative (free, 1/day)
- **Morning Co-pilot** — conversational follow-ups: "what if I move this meeting?" (Pro)
- **Plan My Day / Week** — AI sequences your work around energy and deadlines (Pro)
- **Semantic Search** — find tasks by meaning, not keyword (Pro)
- **MCP Integration** — Claude and OpenClaw can read and write your tasks natively
- **5 languages** with real typographic care: EN, 繁體中文, 简体中文, 日本語, 한국어
- Tasks, calendar sync, habits, Pomodoro, Eisenhower matrix, notes, share groups

**What it doesn't do:**
- Team features (it's personal, by design)
- Gamification (no streaks, no points, no guilt)
- Noise (no red badges, no push notifications about drinking water)
- VC funding (bootstrapped, sustainable)

I built this as a solo maker alongside my day job in Taipei. It's free to start, with Plus ($5/mo) for calendar sync and unlimited Daily Editions, and Pro ($9/mo) for the full AI co-pilot.

The Daily Edition is the thing I'm most proud of. Try it for a week and see if your mornings feel different.

## First 3 Comments (pre-drafted)

**Comment 1 (reply to early question about AI):**
> The AI doesn't just summarize — it prioritizes. It knows that your 2 PM design review needs prep, that you tend to defer emails on Tuesdays, and that your Q3 review draft is blocking three other tasks. It factors all of that into the briefing. The goal is to save you the 10-15 minutes you'd spend just figuring out what to do first.

**Comment 2 (reply to pricing question):**
> Free tier gets you the full task system + 1 Daily Edition per day. Plus ($5/mo) adds two-way Google Calendar sync, unlimited Daily Editions, smart reschedule, and end-of-day reflection. Pro ($9/mo) adds the full AI co-pilot: Plan My Day, Voice → Task, meeting prep, and semantic search. No tricks, no annual-only pricing.

**Comment 3 (reply to privacy question):**
> Your data stays in your Supabase instance (we use row-level security). The AI only sees what it needs to generate the briefing — it doesn't train on your data, and we don't sell it. You can export everything as JSON anytime. We're bootstrapped, so we don't have VCs pushing us to monetize your data.
